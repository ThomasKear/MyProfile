Some of my Projects

Restaurant Roulette

For those days when you don't want to decide

A website that uses RESTful API calls to Google.Maps and Zomato to help decide where to eat. With a push of a button (the roulette wheel) you populate a list (hosted by Googles Firebase) of restaurants within 10 miles of your current location or addres of your choice. It publishes one result at a time on a map with the restaurants information and ratings, and get directions.
